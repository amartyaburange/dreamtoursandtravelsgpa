Places to Visit in the Maldives A Maldives holiday is a marriage of pristine white beaches and lavish hospitality. The romantic ambience of the island country is one of the reasons why it is considered one of the best honeymoon destinations. The Maldives has several interesting places to visit.

Banana Reef


In addition to stunning caves, beautiful coral growths, and majestic cliffs, Banana Reef is one of the oldest reefs discovered in the Maldives. Among the most visited dive destinations globally, the Banana Reef has many sharks, barracudas, and groupers. A gentle current combined with good visibility creates the perfect opportunity for novice divers and learners to get their feet wet.

Male


In addition to its ancient mosque and colourful buildings, Male is one of the smallest capitals in the world. The island has managed to become one of the Maldives' most visited places due to its beauty and progressiveness. This is a casual town, which makes it best to wander without pre planned routes. Male is also home to many heritage buildings.

Artificial Beach


Have you ever attended a carnival? Do you want your trips to have some cultural flair and a youthful vibe? Make sure you have a fabulous experience at Artificial Beach! In North Mal-Atoll young people are often found relaxing and enjoying a dive.

Halaveli Island


There is much to enjoy about the Halaveli islands, including the solitude that one craves, especially after living in a city. More than 57 water villas grace this island, each designed to harmonise with the surrounding natural environment. Guests will also be pampered with elaborate hospitality throughout their stay.
